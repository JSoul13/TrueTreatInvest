About TreatmentConnect AI

TreatmentConnect AI is revolutionizing how patients connect with treatment facilities. Our advanced AI platform qualifies leads, matches patients to the right care, and saves facilities from high marketing costs— all while ensuring ethical, efficient placements.

Partnerships with Corp Human Resources and major insurers (NYShip, BCBS, Cigna, Aetna), we're building a network that prioritizes quality and accessibility.
